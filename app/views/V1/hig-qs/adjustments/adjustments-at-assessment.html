Adjustments at an assessment

We may invite you to a phone, video or face-to-face assessment if we need more information from you.

We can make adjustments to help you access an assessment.

For example, tell us if:

  • you have difficulty hearing
  • you do not have equipment at home for a video call
  • you cannot use stairs
  • you have difficulty travelling or using public transport
  • you need an accessible toilet
